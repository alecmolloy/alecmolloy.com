Bio

I am a junior at Santa Clara University in California, pursuing a Bachelor of Arts degree in Journalism. I am on the editorial staff of two of the University's award-winning publications. I work at The Santa Clara newspaper as the Design Editor, and as the Production Editor and Webmaster at The Santa Clara Review

For the last few years I have worked as a concierge tech support specialist in Fairfield County, CT. I provide on-call support to clients, and am able to help them with any and all issues on a computer or electronic device.

I run the typography blog 'prettyfac.es,' for which I write, edit, and manage the theme for. It features typographical art and news stories, along with features such as interviews with type designers and application developers.

In my free time I enjoy playing and listening to good music, watching classic movies and obsessing over silly things like typefaces. I may get swamped by work at times, but I love the things that I do, and consider myself employed in some of the best jobs ever. However, if you think you've got something better, I'm all ears.
